DO's & DONT's

At Station

Do stand towards the left side of the escalator, while moving keep right.

Do follow instructions in the Lift and give preference to senior citizens and differently abled passengers.

Do stand in queue at Ticket counters, AFC gates and Ticket vending machines.

Evacuate the metro station in case of an emergency.

Limit your baggage to not more than 25 Kg.

Don't carry any Inflammable objects, weapons, pets and heavy luggage to the metro premises.

Don't abuse and misbehave with the metro staff.

Do take care of your valuables and belongings.

Do contact metro staff in case of any suspicious or unattended object is found.

On Platform And Train

Don't rush on the platform while entering the train.

Don't Litter, Spit or Smoke on the platform or inside the coach

Don't throw paper and plastic objects on the platform or inside the coach.

Do stand in queue while waiting for train on the platform.

Do produce QR tickets, CSC, Paper tickets to Metro Personnel for inspection whenever requested.

Do stand behind the yellow line on the platform and follow the signages.

Don't try to open the doors forcefully or lean over the platform.

Don't try to stand on the roof of the train as the overhead wire is energized with 25000 volts.

Do mind the gap between the platform and entrance of the coach while boarding and alighting.

Do give preference to Senior Citizens, Children, Pregnant Women and Differently Abled passengers while boarding and alighting.

Don't access the tracks.
